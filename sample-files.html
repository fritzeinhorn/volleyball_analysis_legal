🎬 Video Files

VolleyRecon supports synchronized video playback alongside scouting data. Due to their large file size, match videos cannot be hosted directly on this page.

Recommended approach for video files:

  • Store your video file locally on your device (e.g., in your Documents folder).
  • In VolleyRecon, use the "Open Video" feature to select the video file after loading the .dvw scouting file.
  • The app will automatically synchronize the video with the scouting data.

Supported video formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and other common formats supported by your operating system's media player.

For large video files, you can share them with your team using cloud storage services such as Google Drive, Dropbox, or iCloud Drive. Simply download the file to your device and open it in VolleyRecon.
